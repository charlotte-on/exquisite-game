Exquisite corpse with a twist

This game is a renewed version of the original game. In addition to words I added an emoji keyboard, the particularity of the emojis is that they will not be hidden. You choose if you want to make it a clue or not, when reading it you can either ignore it or let it influence your writing üòâ

How to play ?

You will have 10 rounds to write you text. Each turn will begin with the last tree words written inside the input to help you write meaningful sentences. You have to submit between 30 and 50 characters, a message will let you know if you're too short or too long. When it's the last round, the "Publish" button will turn into a "Reveal" Button.
